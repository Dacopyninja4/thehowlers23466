    About Us

    Here at SecureStash, we have many mission and goals. First of all, we want to create a secure and accessible platform where people can save and send their family information after they die. Second, we want to make this a completely free application to help provide our service to everyone in the world.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to create a secure and acessible community for people to send their family their passwords, notes, files, and more after they die.

    Our Plan

    We want to make an application where a user can add the emails of their family members and save passwords, files, and notes. When the user dies, we can send their family any important information that user wants them to know.

    Our Vision

    A secure and safe community where users can rely on our application send their families the information they deserve.

    Services

    Here is a brief overview of what we provided here at SecureStash

    Password Saving

    Save the passwords and usernames to any application that you want your family to know about.

    Files

    Have any important files you would like to share to your family? Easily share them using our application.

    Notes

    Easily send any information, notes, and secrets through our note transfer feature.

    Adding Family

    Add family members using their email to let us know where to send your information.

    Security

    No need to worry about security as all of your information is encrypted and stored in a database.

    Verification

    Every 2 weeks, a confirmation email will be sent to you. If you don't respond or you say that you want to transfer your data, a 2 week hold will be placed before a one month's notice before your data will be fully transferred. This will include a 2 factor authentication process as well.
